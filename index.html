About Me

I am a young entrepreneur who loves the arts and is passionate about technology and innovation, willing to unite them to adapt to the constant change in our society.                                  At the same time, in the near future, I seek to merge design and technology for the implementation of video games and interactive design.

I consider myself a leader who works very well in a team, I have an authoritarian character when the situation warrants it, creative and responsible.
